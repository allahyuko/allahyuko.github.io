About Me

I am Hanchen Jiang, an associate professor in the School of Government, University of International Business and Economics.

I did my post-doc project in the School of Public Policy and Management, Tsinghua University from 2019 to 2022, where I cooperated with Prof. Angang Hu and Dr. Xiao Tang.

In 2019, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Management Science and Engineering from Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Maoshan Qiang. In 2013, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Hydraulic Engineering from Tsinghua University.
